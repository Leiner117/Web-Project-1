Colosseum

The Flavian Amphitheater, better known as the Colosseum, is one of the most iconic landmarks of Ancient Rome. Built in the 1st century AD, it hosted gladiator fights, public spectacles, and remains one of the most visited monuments in the world.

Opening Hours:

8:30 AM – 7:00 PM (varies by season)

Entry Fee:

€16 (includes access to the Roman Forum)

Official Website:

colosseo.it

Included with Colosseum ticket
